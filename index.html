KNITTING

  • We are capable of producing 3000 kilograms of fabric per day with our in-house facility.

Dyeing

  • Out sourcing from PARTNERSHIP BASED Dyeing & Processing Mills for the past 10 years with Consistent Quality.
  • All our products comply to REACH standards Imported high end machines Balloon Padding and Squeezing Machine,Tensionless Relax Dier
